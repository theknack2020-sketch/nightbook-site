What it is

Nightbook is a privacy-first dream journal for iPhone and iPad. Tap once to type, or hold the microphone and whisper — your voice is transcribed on your device. Three hundred curated symbols from folklore and literature. A pattern view that unfolds the more you log. Everything stays on your device.

Features

Private by design

Every word stays on your device. No server reads it.

Voice capture

Whisper half-asleep. On-device transcription.

300 symbols

Folklore, literature, reflective prompts. No predictions.

Patterns unfold

Notice what keeps coming back, over weeks and months.

Widgets

Today's symbol and your streak on the home screen.

iPad-optimized

Full-canvas sidebar layout on larger screens.
