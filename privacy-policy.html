Privacy Policy

Effective: April 18, 2026

The short version:

Nightbook writes your dreams to your device. We never see them. No server reads them. No analytics tracks what you write. If you turn on iCloud sync, it's your private iCloud — encrypted end-to-end by Apple. We don't track you across apps or websites.

1. Who we are

Nightbook ("the app", "we", "us") is developed and distributed by TheKnack. Questions or concerns: nightbook.support@theknack.dev.

2. What stays on your device

Every dream entry, every voice recording, every tag, and every symbol you favourite lives in Nightbook's local storage on your iPhone or iPad. We do not upload, mirror, or read this content. The app has no network call that sends journal text off the device.

  • Voice recordings are transcribed by Apple's on-device Speech framework. Audio never leaves your device.
  • Pattern analysis runs entirely in memory on your device.
  • AI reflections on iOS 26+ with Apple Intelligence use Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework. Nothing is sent to a server.

3. iCloud sync (optional, you control it)

If you enable iCloud sync in Settings → Pro → iCloud Sync, your dreams are stored in your private iCloud container through Apple's CloudKit. Apple encrypts this container end-to-end and holds the keys on your devices. We, TheKnack, have no access to that data. If you turn sync off, your dreams stay only on the device where you wrote them.

4. What we do collect (only the minimum)

Nightbook sends anonymous, non-identifying product telemetry to TelemetryDeck — a privacy-respecting analytics provider. The only data points we emit are:

  • App launches (a count, nothing more)
  • Screen views — names of tabs opened
  • Feature usage events — e.g. "voice capture started", "paywall shown" (no content, just that it happened)
  • Crash data — stack traces that help us fix bugs
  • Performance metrics — launch time, memory footprint

None of this data includes the text of your dreams, your tags, your voice recordings, your Apple ID, your email, or any way to identify you personally. TelemetryDeck receives anonymised signals only. Read their privacy stance: telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers.
  • We do not show the App Tracking Transparency prompt — there is nothing to track.
  • We do not integrate third-party SDKs beyond TelemetryDeck.

6. App Store purchases

If you purchase Nightbook Pro (monthly, yearly, or lifetime), Apple processes the transaction. We receive a receipt from Apple that confirms your subscription status; we do not receive your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

7. Microphone and Speech Recognition

To use voice capture, Nightbook asks permission for the microphone and on-device Speech Recognition. You can revoke these permissions anytime in Settings → Nightbook. Audio is recorded only while you hold the microphone button, and transcription is performed locally using Apple's on-device speech engine.

8. Notifications

Nightbook can send a single gentle morning reminder if you enable notifications. These are scheduled locally on your device via Apple's notification system; no push server is involved. You can change or disable the reminder anytime in Settings → Me → Morning reminder.

9. Children

Nightbook is rated 12+ on the App Store. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Your rights

Because your journal content lives on your device, you already have full control:

  • Access: open the app, everything is there.
  • Delete: remove entries in the app, or delete the app entirely — all local data goes with it.
  • Portability: export features land in Nightbook v1.1; reach out if you need your data sooner.

For GDPR / CCPA / other jurisdictions: the anonymous analytics we collect do not constitute personal data under most definitions. If you would like us to delete any data associated with your device's anonymous ID, email nightbook.support@theknack.dev.

11. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Nightbook handles data, this page will update with a new effective date. Substantive changes will also be surfaced in-app on your next launch.

12. Not a medical or diagnostic service

Nightbook is a reflective journaling and learning tool. It is not a medical, psychological, or diagnostic service. Symbol entries draw from folklore, literature, and dream traditions — they are invitations to reflect, not clinical interpretations. If you are experiencing recurring distressing dreams, please speak with a licensed mental health professional.

13. Governing law

This policy and the operation of Nightbook are governed by the laws of the Republic of Turkey. Where European Union consumer protections (including GDPR) provide stronger rights, those rights apply to EU residents.
